I will learn and work with Node.js, Express, and MongoDB to develop a full-stack understanding. These certifications will not only bolster my resume but also provide structured learning paths. Building a personal brand is crucial for career growth. I will create a portfolio showcasing my projects, write technical articles, and engage with the tech community on platforms like LinkedIn, GitHub, and X. This will help me establish my presence in the tech industry and connect with like-minded professionals.
